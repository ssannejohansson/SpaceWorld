About SpaceWorld

Since 2002 our space themed amusement park has brought thousands of people together to enjoy the universe we've created. At SpaceWorld you can experience everything from extraordinary food to adventures out of this world.

Our Experiences

Beyond food and adventures, SpaceWorld's large outdoor arena offers live performances as concerts, theaters and smaller musical events. Next to the amusement park you'll find our own space themed hotel, which also includes an event and conference center for corporate events.

Our Values

We want SpaceWorld to be a place where everyone can be themselves and where you can escape reality for a while. With our core values Safety, Consideration, Quality, Joy and Creativity we promise you a great, carefree time at SpaceWorld.
